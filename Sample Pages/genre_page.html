[close] The first of its kind, Michaela Smalley's Women's Sports Book Club is dedicated to growing awareness and support for women's sports and women in sport. We read one book per month but encourage readers to read at their own pace. Women's sports, diversity in sport, women working in sport, or sport business is reflected in each and every book we read. Readers are also encouraged to participate in the discussion board that corresponds with the book of the month. The more people we have participate in the discussion, the better we can connect and learn from each other. All of us bring unique perspectives and experiences to the table, so this is a great way to enhance the entire experience.
